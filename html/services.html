Services

Individual Therapy

Working on a one-to-one basis, some people feel more comfortable sharing and exploring in a space where there is only themselves and the therapist.

Each therapy session is 50 mins, takes place once a week for one session at the same place. I provide sessions both in-person or online. The sessions can be for short-term or long-term depending on your requirement.

I find having a review every 6-session useful so we can look back observe progress and discuss anything we can further work on.

Before we start, I can offer a free 30 mins consultation with you to talk about your goals, what art therapy is, how the session works, your preference, you can ask me any questions you have and then we can decide whether we would be a good fit working together.

Fee: ¬£60 per session (This price includes time for note writing, session preparation and materials used in the session.)

Group Therapy

Working in a group of 6-8 people (or 4-6 for children and young people) is a helpful way to explore personal or common themes alongside others. Through creating and reflecting on images together, we learn from each other and support each other. It is also helpful to learn some social skills.

Each group session lasts 60-90 minutes, takes place once a week, and usually continues for 8-12 weeks. It could be shorter or longer as an ongoing group.

Before we start the group, I will ensure members understand what to expect in the group session and the ground rules for the session.

Open Studio

Open studio session is a type of group work, that does not require referrals and provides a space for individuals to drop-in and create art freely. The session is facilitated by an art therapist, and ensure a safe-space for art making and discussion.

Other services

Creative Workshops

I can offer creative workshops that incorporate reflecting on art to think about different themes such as work, loss, well-being, self-care, team building. I can also offer reflective groups to support staff in reflecting on work together.

Discuss your needs with Ann

I am open to discuss different services, charge, and the needs of your organisation and service user with you. We can work toward finding the best services for your organisation.
